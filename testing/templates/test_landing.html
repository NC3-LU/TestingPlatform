Email Security Tools

These tools are built to give you an easy way of configuring DMARC for your emails. DMARC (Domain-based Message Authentication, Reporting and Conformance) allows people you send mails to to ascertain that you are the actual sender, thus protecting against identity usurpation.

SPF (Sender Policy Framework) is, like DMARC, a system of email validation, which allows you to publish a list of mail servers that are allowed to send emails in your name. It is best used in parallel with DMARC.
